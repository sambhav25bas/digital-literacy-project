## ***UPI Fraud Prevention Tips***

* Never reveal your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details to anyone.
* Don’t scan unknown QR codes to receive funds.
* Use strong passwords and activate two-factor authentication for banking apps.
* Don’t click suspicious links or install unknown apps.
* Check your banking transaction history and SMS messages frequently.
* Block your mobile and bank account immediately in case your mobile gets lost or stolen.
* Only install apps from trusted sources like the app store.
* Don’t fall for fake calls from your bank or customer support.

#### **Reporting Channels**

If you become a victim of UPI fraud, report on:

* Website: https://cybercrime.gov.in
* Helpline Number: 1930
